Tips for a “Pleasant Trip”:

  • Silence is the golden rule .

    Maintanance of silence is mandatory in birdsanctuary around places. Blowing of horns, playing loud music are prohibited in the birdsanctuary because it may harm the birds.

  • Do not carry firearms.

    They may be confiscate.they may affect the birds.firearms are completly prohibited around the area.

  • Don’t litter the sanctuary.

    Use dustbins and always carry your leftovers back. cleanness and maintanance of the birdsanctuary is important so please co-operate to wellness of the sanctuary

  • Do not disturb birds.

    Be inquisitive and develop an eye for birds. the birds are very senstive around the nature.the sanctuary provide freedom for the birds so dont harm the birds.

  • Be sensitive to the needs of other visitors.

    you may have to treat other visitors to good dont make noise with other visitors treat them as your friend.

  • Visit within the specified timings.

    Entry beyond these timings is treated as trespass and may invite legal action..
